1. Product & Production Requirements

Module Architecture

  • Configuration: 13S9P (117 cells)
  • Cell Type: 18650 Cylindrical Li-ion
  • Electricals: 46.8 V (Nom) / 54.6 V (Max)
  • Capacity: 27 Ah nominal

Manufacturing Targets

  • Annual Volume: 150000 units / year
  • Operation: 2 shifts / 220 working days
  • Output Target: 682 modules / day
  • Throughput: 39897 cells per shift

The high cell-per-shift volume requires automated feeding, orientation control, and 100% data traceability to ensure repeatable quality standards.

3. Module Electrical Characteristics

Nominal Voltage 46.8 V
Max Voltage 54.6 V

Design Criteria

  • Automated Feeding & Grading
  • Low thermal distortion joining
  • 100% Traceability
  • Integrated Thermal Management

Design Implications

The electrical parameters mandate strict insulation and continuity testing within the cycle time constraint. Throughput justifies high-precision automated orientation to prevent polarity errors during the assembly of 117 cells per module.

4. Manufacturing Concept

Production Strategy

Establishing a semi-automated linear line to balance efficiency with operational flexibility.

  • Automated: Cell placement, laser welding, inline inspection.
  • Semi-Automated: Fixture preparation, mechanical housing.
  • Flow: One-piece flow with integrated buffer zones.

Process Pillars

  • High-speed robotic cell orientation
  • Laser welding for low thermal impact
  • 100% vision-based polarity verification
  • Real-time MES data logging

7. Quality Control Strategy

Inline Process Controls

  • Cell sorting verification.
  • Vision systems for polarity detection.
  • Weld monitoring for beam consistency.
  • Presence checks for thermal barbs & structural parts.

End-of-Line Validation

  • High-Voltage (HV) isolation resistance.
  • Module terminal voltage verification.
  • Electrical continuity across series-parallel path.
  • Short functional load test.
